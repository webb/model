jp3-52:AirspaceCoordinatingMeasureTypeAndUsageType

complexType AirspaceCoordinatingMeasureTypeAndUsageType in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/codes/jp3-52/4.0/

Definition

A data type for a measure(s) employed to facilitate the efficient use of airspace to accomplish missions and simultaneously provide safeguards for friendly forces. Also called ACMs.
