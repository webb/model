cbrn:RadEncounterDeviceToShieldingDistanceMeasure

element RadEncounterDeviceToShieldingDistanceMeasure in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/cbrn/4.1/

Definition

A distance from the center of mass of a encounter device to the closest outside portion of the outermost layer of shielding that is found between the encounter device and the radiation source.
