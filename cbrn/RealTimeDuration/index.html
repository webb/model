cbrn:RealTimeDuration

element RealTimeDuration in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/cbrn/4.1/

Definition

A total clock time (in ISO 8601 format) expended by an instrument in collecting a measurement; the duration shall be greater than zero.
