cbrn:ConveyanceSecondaryColorCode

element ConveyanceSecondaryColorCode in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/cbrn/4.1/

Definition

A color that identifies a lower-most or rear-most color of a two-tone conveyance or a lesser color of a multi-colored conveyance.
