cbrn:RadInstrumentCharacteristics

element RadInstrumentCharacteristics in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/cbrn/4.1/

Definition

A radiation measurement instrument's characteristics that are not otherwise explicitly addressed in this standard. Each non-standard characteristic consists of name, value, units, and value data class. Characteristics may also be organized in character
