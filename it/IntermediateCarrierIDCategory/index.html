it:IntermediateCarrierIDCategory

element IntermediateCarrierIDCategory in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/internationalTrade/4.1/

Definition

A coded ID for a transit (in-bond) movement, the identity of the carrier's which transport the goods from consignor to exporting carrier and from importing carrier to consignee. A party providing the transport of goods between named points.
