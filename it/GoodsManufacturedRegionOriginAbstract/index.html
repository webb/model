element GoodsManufacturedRegionOriginAbstract in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/internationalTrade/4.1/

Definition

A data concept for a region in which the goods have been produced or manufactured, according to criteria laid down for the purposes of application of the Customs tariff, or quantitative restrictions, or of any other measure related to trade.
