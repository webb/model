it:TransportContractDocumentType

complexType TransportContractDocumentType in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/internationalTrade/4.1/

A data type for a contract between multiple parties pertaining to cargo shipments e.g. export shipment with a carrier i.e. bill of lading(s) or manifest.
