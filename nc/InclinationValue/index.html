nc:InclinationValue

element InclinationValue in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/niem-core/4.0/

Definition

An angle of rotation of a thing about its lateral axis, measured in degrees between the longitudinal axis and the horizontal plane; negative values indicate a downward rotation.
