j:ChargeSeverityDescriptionText

element ChargeSeverityDescriptionText in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.1/

Definition

A description of the level of the offense such as Grade A Misdemeanor, Gross Misdemeanor, Aggravated Misdemeanor. States may transmit numeric grade levels (1, 2, 3) or define degrees alphanumerically (A, B, C.)
