j:ReferralDueDate

element ReferralDueDate in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.1/

Definition

A date that is calculated from the referral constraint element in days where the Supervisor of Record has to refer by a certain date due to the nature of the risk for the subject being in Custody.
