j:CourtOrderDesignatedSubject

element CourtOrderDesignatedSubject in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.1/

Definition

A person to which a court order applies. The subject could be a Protected Party, the recipient of a Summons or Subpoena, the subject of a Warrant, etc.
