j:SentenceComputationTotalInoperativeDayQuantity

element SentenceComputationTotalInoperativeDayQuantity in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.1/

Definition

A number of days, or portions thereof, that a subject is no longer in official detention for reasons such as escape, civil contempt, or release pending appeal. This time does not count toward satisfaction of sentence.
