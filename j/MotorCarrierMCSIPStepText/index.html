j:MotorCarrierMCSIPStepText

element MotorCarrierMCSIPStepText in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.1/

Definition

A description of the Motor Carrier Safety Improvement Process (MCSIP) Step, reflecting the phase of the MCSIP program that the carrier is assigned to. The step is assigned based on safety statistics (SAFESTAT).
