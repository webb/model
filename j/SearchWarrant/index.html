j:SearchWarrant

element SearchWarrant in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.1/

Definition

An order signed by a judge authorizing a search of a place or a vehicle. The search warrant is issued for an address where things which may be evidence are to be found.
