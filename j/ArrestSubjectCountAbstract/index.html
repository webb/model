j:ArrestSubjectCountAbstract

element ArrestSubjectCountAbstract in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.1/

Definition

A data concept for an indication of whether or not to count or score an arrested subject in a system to avoid duplicate counts in the event that the subject may be a part of multiple arrests.
