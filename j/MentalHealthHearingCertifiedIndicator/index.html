j:MentalHealthHearingCertifiedIndicator

element MentalHealthHearingCertifiedIndicator in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.1/

Definition

True if the hearing is a Certification Hearing and that the parolee has a right to a jury trial in the Superior Court if the BPH finds during the certification hearing that the parolee meets the requirements of the MDO law; false otherwise.
