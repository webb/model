j:AppellateCase

element AppellateCase in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.1/

Definition

A single case heard by the Court of Appeals (Intermediate Court of Appeal) or Supreme Court (The Court of Last Resort). This case does not retry the original case but determines whether the original case was tried properly and the defendant
