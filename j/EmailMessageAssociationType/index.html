j:EmailMessageAssociationType

complexType EmailMessageAssociationType in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.1/

Definition

A data type for a relationship between originator, receiver, and an e-mail message. Originator and receiver can be a person or an organization.
