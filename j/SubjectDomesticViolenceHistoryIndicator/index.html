j:SubjectDomesticViolenceHistoryIndicator

element SubjectDomesticViolenceHistoryIndicator in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.1/

Definition

True if subject has been arrested for or convicted of an offense involving the infliction of physical injury or the creation of a reasonable fear that physical injury or harm will be inflicted, by a parent or member or former member of the household; false otherwise.
