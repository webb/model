j:FirearmType

complexType FirearmType in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.1/

Definition

A data type for a weapon that will, or is designed to, or may readily be converted to expel a projectile by air, carbon dioxide, or the action of an explosive.
