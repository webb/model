j:PretrialInvestigation

element PretrialInvestigation in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.1/

Definition

An investigation occurring prior to trial that assesses the likelihood that a subject will fail to appear in court and the danger to the community posed by a subject.
