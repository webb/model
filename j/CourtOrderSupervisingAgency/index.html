j:CourtOrderSupervisingAgency

element CourtOrderSupervisingAgency in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.1/

Definition

An agency responsible for evaluating, investigating, supervising and/or setting conditions for a subject incarcerated, on probation, conditional release, supervised release, parole, or otherwise under the jurisdiction of the court, the Department of Corrections, or a Community Corrections Act county.
