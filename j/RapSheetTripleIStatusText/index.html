j:RapSheetTripleIStatusText

element RapSheetTripleIStatusText in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.1/

Definition

A status based on the Interstate Identification Index system (III - "Triple I"), a cooperative federal-state system for the exchange of criminal history records maintained in the criminal history repositories of the states and the FBI. When a query on a subject's criminal history is submitted to III, a response record from one or more states and/or the FBI will be transmitted immediately, or an acknowledgment with a notice of when the record will be provided. Allowable values Multi-state, Unknown, and CA only (example state).
