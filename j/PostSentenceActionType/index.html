j:PostSentenceActionType

complexType PostSentenceActionType in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.1/

Definition

A data type for an optional Post-Sentence Segment in the Criminal History record in order to transmit actions following adjudication and sentencing, such as a pardon, certificate of relief, restoration of rights, retirement, clemency, or commutatio
