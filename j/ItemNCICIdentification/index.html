j:ItemNCICIdentification

element ItemNCICIdentification in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/jxdm/6.1/

Definition

An identification of an item which is self-checking and consists of an alphabetic character followed by nine numeric characters automatically assigned by the National Crime Information Center System to each accepted record.
