st:EndingRampTerminalLocationID

element EndingRampTerminalLocationID in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/surfaceTransportation/4.1/

Definition

An identifier of a location on the roadway at the ending ramp terminal (e.g. route-milepost for that roadway) if the ramp connects with a roadway at that point.
