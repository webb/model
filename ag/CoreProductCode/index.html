ag:CoreProductCode

element CoreProductCode in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/agriculture/4.0/

Definition

A product code providing a classification of agricultural products defined by the Pilot CVT (Crop Validation Table). Products are classified into individual crop and commodity types, tree types, and types of livestock.
