ag:AcreageSubmissionFile

element AcreageSubmissionFile in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/agriculture/4.0/

Definition

A file that is submitted to the crop acreage clearinghouse providing a detailed report regarding the crops planted on a farm, itemized down to the acreage of individual fields and subfields and the individual producer stakeholders.
