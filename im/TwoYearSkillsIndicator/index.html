im:TwoYearSkillsIndicator

element TwoYearSkillsIndicator in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/immigration/4.1/

Definition

True if the Exchange Visitor (EV) is subject to a two year home-country physical presence requirement due to EVs position being on the short supply skill-list within EVs home country; false otherwise.
