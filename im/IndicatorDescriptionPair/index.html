element IndicatorDescriptionPair in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/immigration/4.1/

Definition

A construct for the mutliple parts of a complex question that specifies: if a question needed to be answered, if True/Yes, False/No was provided as an answer, and the text of the question.
