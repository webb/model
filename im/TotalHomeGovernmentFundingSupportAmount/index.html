im:TotalHomeGovernmentFundingSupportAmount

element TotalHomeGovernmentFundingSupportAmount in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/immigration/4.1/

Definition

A number indicating the funding amount provided by the Exchange Visitors (EV) home government to the Exchange Visitor, in order to support his/her participation within a designated EV Program.
