m:VesselCargoCategoryText

element VesselCargoCategoryText in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/maritime/4.1/

Definition

A kind of cargo that a vessel is carrying. Cargo types may be categorized as DG=Dangerous Goods, HS=Harmful Substances, or MP=Marine Pollutants.
