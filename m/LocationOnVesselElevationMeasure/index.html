m:LocationOnVesselElevationMeasure

element LocationOnVesselElevationMeasure in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/maritime/4.1/

Definition

A measure of the elevation above or below the waterline of a vessel. A positive value implies a location above the waterline and a negative value implies a location below the waterline.
