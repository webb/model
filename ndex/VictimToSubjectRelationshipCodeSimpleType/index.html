ndex:VictimToSubjectRelationshipCodeSimpleType

simpleType VictimToSubjectRelationshipCodeSimpleType in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/codes/fbi_ndex/4.1/

Definition

A data type for a code that identifies the victim's relationship to subject who perpetrated a crime against them, depicting who the victim was to the offender.
