ndex:PretrialReleaseStatusCodeSimpleType

simpleType PretrialReleaseStatusCodeSimpleType in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/codes/fbi_ndex/4.1/

Definition

A data type for a code that identifies the release status of the subject of pre-trial investigation.

Diagram

Enumerations

ValueDefinition
ACCEPTThe subject will be conditionally released / released to supervision without bail being required.
ACCEPT BAIL_BONDThe subject will be conditionally released / released to supervision and bail or bond is stipulated as a part of the subjects conditional release.
REJECTThe subject is a poor risk for release and/or a risk to not appear and/or likely to re-offend, thus Pre-Trial release is rejected and the subject will not be released.
