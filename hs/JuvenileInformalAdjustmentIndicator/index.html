hs:JuvenileInformalAdjustmentIndicator

element JuvenileInformalAdjustmentIndicator in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/humanServices/4.1/

Definition

True if the juvenile (with parental consent) admits the facts of the alleged delinquent act, and the tribunal approves the conditions of the juvenile's supervision for a period of time without being adjudicated; false otherwise.
