hs:SubstanceAbusePastDescriptionText

element SubstanceAbusePastDescriptionText in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/humanServices/4.1/

Definition

A description of past substance abuse issues which may include details about substances used or abused in the past, past treatment episodes or other details related to a person's substance use in the past.
