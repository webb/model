hs:SurrogateParentChildAssociation

element SurrogateParentChildAssociation in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/humanServices/4.1/

Definition

An association between a child and a person who participated in one or more phases of the child's conception and gestation (for example, egg donor, sperm donor, maternal surrogate).
