hs:ChildSupportNonTANFArrearsAmount

element ChildSupportNonTANFArrearsAmount in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/humanServices/4.1/

Definition

A portion of total arrears identified in the child-support order as being attributable to child-support payments that were due from the obligor in the past but were not paid.
