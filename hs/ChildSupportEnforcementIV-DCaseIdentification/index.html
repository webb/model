hs:ChildSupportEnforcementIV-DCaseIdentification

element ChildSupportEnforcementIV-DCaseIdentification in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/humanServices/4.1/

Definition

A unique identification assigned by a local agency to identify and track each IV-D case in the state, tribe, or territory ("IV-D" refers to Title IV-D of the Social Security Act).
