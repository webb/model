hs:JuvenileSpecialNeedAbstract

element JuvenileSpecialNeedAbstract in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/humanServices/4.1/

Definition

A data concept for describing the characteristics that states might define as special needs, which qualify a child or youth's adoption for a subsidy under Title IV-E of the Social Security Act.
