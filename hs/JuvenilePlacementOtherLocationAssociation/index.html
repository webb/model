hs:JuvenilePlacementOtherLocationAssociation

element JuvenilePlacementOtherLocationAssociation in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/humanServices/4.1/

Definition

An association between a juvenile and a location -- other than a foster home, kin's residence, congregate care, or other facility -- where the juvenile is directed to reside.
