ag_codes:PlantedDateModifiedOtherReasonTextSimpleType

simpleType PlantedDateModifiedOtherReasonTextSimpleType in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/codes/ag_codes/4.0/

Definition

A data type for free form text providing the reason that a planted date was modified from its original value when a reason other than the possible list of provided reasons is needed.
