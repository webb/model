biom:NominalTransmittingResolutionValue

element NominalTransmittingResolutionValue in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/biometrics/4.1/

Definition

A transaction's transmitting resolution, in pixels per mm. Valid value between 19.29 and 20.08 OR equal to 00.00 (corresponds to 500ppi postive and negative 2% ) OR between 38.58 and 40.16 (corresponds to 1000ppi postive and negative 2%.) Shoul
