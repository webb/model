biom:VocalContentInformation

element VocalContentInformation in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/biometrics/4.1/

Definition

A kind of field that indicates whether the voice recording content has been diarized to indicate the speech segments of interest pertaining to the subject of this voice record.
