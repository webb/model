biom:SpeakerPluralityCode

element SpeakerPluralityCode in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/biometrics/4.1/

Definition

A kind of second information item is the speaker plurality code / SPC. It is optional and indicates plurality of speakers represented on voice recording: M = multiple speakers S = single speaker.
