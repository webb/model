biom:RecordingContentCommentText

element RecordingContentCommentText in namespace http://release.niem.gov/niem/domains/biometrics/4.1/

Definition

A kind of comment that allows statements of up to 4000 Unicode characters in length describing methods by which the plurality and nationality of the speakers was determined, along with any supporting text.
